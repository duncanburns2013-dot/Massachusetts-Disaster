# PART 1: THE CENSUS GAME

## How Congressional Representation Actually Works

The 14th Amendment mandates counting "the whole number of persons in each state" for congressional apportionment. This includes:

- âœ… Citizens
- âœ… Legal permanent residents
- âœ… Visa holders
- âœ… Undocumented immigrants
- âœ… Anyone present on Census Day

**The result:** States can gain or keep congressional seats by having large non-citizen populations, regardless of how many actual voters live there.
