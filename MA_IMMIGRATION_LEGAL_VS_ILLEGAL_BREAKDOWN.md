## üìã **DETAILED BREAKDOWN BY PROGRAM**

### **1. EMERGENCY SHELTER: $900M/year**

**Status Breakdown:** ‚ùì **UNKNOWN - State Refuses to Publish Data**

**What We Know:**
- Total families served (peak): 7,600
- Current families (Dec 2025): 2,047
- Cost per family: $3,870/week

**What We DON'T Know:**
- How many are U.S. citizens?
- How many are illegal immigrants?
- How many are asylum seekers (temporary legal status)?
- How many are refugees (legal status)?

**Conflicting Claims:**
- **MA Administration:** "About 50% are U.S. citizens"
- **Local Officials:** "Only 3-4% are U.S. citizens at our sites"
- **Truth:** State REFUSES to publish comprehensive immigration status data

**Who's Included:**
- ‚úÖ U.S. citizens who fell on hard times
- ‚úÖ Legal immigrants (green card holders)
- ‚úÖ Refugees (legal status)
- ‚úÖ Asylum seekers (temporary legal status while case pending)
- ‚úÖ Parolees (temporary legal status under Biden policies)
- ‚úÖ Illegal immigrants
- ‚úÖ Inadmissible migrants

**Best Estimate:**
- Illegal/Inadmissible: 50-70% = **$450M - $630M**
- Legal immigrants/refugees: 10-20% = **$90M - $180M**
- U.S. citizens: 10-30% = **$90M - $270M**

**Source:** MA EOHLC (official data), Local reporting (estimates)

---

### **2. EDUCATION: $575M/year**

**Status Breakdown:** ‚úì **SPECIFICALLY ILLEGAL/INADMISSIBLE**

**What CIS Estimated:**
- "25,000 children who are illegal and inadmissible migrants"
- Cost per pupil: $23,000/year
- **Total: $575 million/year**
- 40% arrived under Biden policies (2021-2024)

**This DOES include:**
- ‚úÖ Children who entered illegally
- ‚úÖ Children of illegal immigrants
- ‚úÖ Unaccompanied minors (8,500 since 2021)
- ‚úÖ Children with asylum cases pending (inadmissible)

**This does NOT include:**
- ‚ùå Children of legal immigrants
- ‚ùå Children of refugees
- ‚ùå Children of naturalized citizens
- ‚ùå U.S.-born children (even if parents are illegal)

**Note:** There are ALSO ~25,000 U.S.-born children of illegal immigrants in MA schools. These are counted as U.S. citizens, NOT immigrants.

**Illegal/Inadmissible Only:** **$575M** ‚úì

**Source:** Center for Immigration Studies analysis, MA DESE data

---

### **3. MASSHEALTH: $450M/year**

**Status Breakdown:** üî∂ **MIXED - Legal and Illegal**

**Total Immigrant Enrollees (Estimate):** ~750,000

**By Status:**

**Legal Immigrants (~600,000):**
- Green card holders after 5 years: Full coverage
- Refugees: Immediate full coverage
- Asylees: Full coverage
- Certain humanitarian statuses: Full coverage
- **Estimated cost: ~$300M/year**

**Illegal/Inadmissible (~150,000):**
- Emergency MassHealth only (limited)
- EXCEPTION: Pregnant women (full coverage regardless of status)
- EXCEPTION: Children under 21 (full coverage regardless of status)
- Emergency room visits (never denied)
- **Estimated cost: ~$150M/year**

**Why Estimates?**
- MassHealth does NOT comprehensively track immigration status
- This is DELIBERATE policy
- Cannot get exact numbers

**Illegal/Inadmissible Portion:** **~$150M** (estimate)

**Source:** MassHealth enrollment data, policy analysis

---

### **4. SNAP/WELFARE: $200M/year**

**Status Breakdown:** üî∂ **MOSTLY LEGAL IMMIGRANTS**

**Who's Eligible:**

**Legal Immigrants:**
- Green card holders after 5 years: SNAP eligible
- Refugees: SNAP eligible immediately
- Asylees: SNAP eligible
- Certain humanitarian statuses: Eligible
- Children: Always eligible (regardless of parents' status)
- **Estimated: ~$150M/year**

**Illegal Immigrants:**
- Generally NOT eligible for SNAP (federal law)
- EXCEPTION: Some states have pilot programs
- EXCEPTION: Can receive benefits on behalf of eligible children
- MA allocated $6M in 2023 for certain inadmissible migrants (7 months only)
- **Estimated: ~$50M/year**

**Cash Assistance (TAFDC, EAEDC, SSI):**
- Similar rules: Mostly legal immigrants eligible
- Illegal immigrants: Very limited eligibility

**The 2026 "Fiscal Time Bomb":**
- 10,500 parolees (currently inadmissible) become eligible after 5 years
- Starting 2026-2028
- Additional $4.6M/year per cohort (SNAP alone)

**Illegal/Inadmissible Portion:** **~$50M** (current, estimate)

**Source:** MA DTA, CIS analysis

---

### **5. HEALTH SAFETY NET: $90M/year**

**Status Breakdown:** üî∂ **MIXED - All Eligible**

**What It Is:**
- Covers uninsured/underinsured for hospital/community health center care
- NO immigration status requirement
- Available to ALL Massachusetts residents

**Who Uses It:**
- Legal immigrants without insurance
- Illegal immigrants (emergency care)
- U.S. citizens without insurance

**Total Program Cost:** ~$300M/year

**Immigrant Share (Est.):** 30-40% = **$90M - $120M/year**

**By Status (Estimates):**
- Illegal/inadmissible: ~60-70% of immigrant share = **~$60M**
- Legal immigrants: ~30-40% of immigrant share = **~$30M**

**Illegal/Inadmissible Portion:** **~$60M** (estimate)

**Source:** Health Safety Net program data, usage estimates

---

### **6. CRIMINAL JUSTICE: $27M/year**

**Status Breakdown:** ‚úì **SPECIFICALLY ILLEGAL IMMIGRANTS**

**What CIS Found:**
- Criminal illegal aliens: 3% of MA corrections population (FY22)
- Cost of incarceration: **$27 million/year**
- This is ONLY for:
  - Convicted of serious or multiple crimes
  - Currently incarcerated
  - NOT including pre-trial detention
  - NOT including probation/parole costs
  - NOT including court costs

**This does NOT include:**
- Legal immigrants who commit crimes (counted as legal residents)
- Illegal immigrants in pre-trial detention
- Illegal immigrants on probation/parole

**Illegal Immigrants Only:** **$27M** ‚úì

**Source:** MA Department of Corrections data (FY22), CIS analysis

---

### **7. MUNICIPAL AID: $30M/year**

**Status Breakdown:** ‚ùì **UNKNOWN - Mixed Purposes**

**What's Included:**
- Reimbursements to cities/towns hosting shelters
- School district emergency aid
- Room occupancy tax reimbursements

**Total (2-year average):** $61.6M √∑ 2 years = **$30.8M/year**

**Cannot Separate By Status:**
- Shelter aid goes to municipalities, not individuals
- No breakdown by immigration status of families served

**Source:** MA EOHLC official data
