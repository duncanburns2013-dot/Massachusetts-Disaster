# PART 12: SOLUTIONS ‚Äî $3.5 BILLION+ IN SAVINGS
üìä **See: `charts/solutions_savings.svg`**

## These reforms require POLITICAL WILL, not new taxes.

### SOLUTION 1: End the Shelter Crisis
**Current Cost: $1+ Billion/year | SAVES: $700M+**

- ‚ùå End right-to-shelter for non-residents and those without legal status
- ‚ùå Strictly enforce 9-month stay limits
- ‚ùå Require proof of work authorization for continued benefits
- ‚ùå Coordinate with federal immigration enforcement
- ‚ùå Return to pre-crisis capacity of 4,100 families

**Massachusetts is the only state with a right-to-shelter law.** Repeal or limit it.

### SOLUTION 2: Overtime Abuse Crackdown
**Current OT: $599M | SAVES: $200M+**

- Cap overtime at 10% of departmental payroll
- Conduct hire vs. overtime cost-benefit analysis
- Special audit of Suffolk Sheriff (27.4% OT)
- Require IG approval for OT exceeding 15%
- Publish all overtime by employee online

### SOLUTION 3: Pension Reform
**Unfunded: $42B | SAVES: $500M+/year**

- Move ALL new hires to hybrid 401k/defined benefit plan
- End pension spiking via final-year overtime padding
- Raise minimum retirement age to 62
- Calculate benefits on career average, not final years
- Cap maximum pension at $100,000 (COLA-adjusted)

### SOLUTION 4: MassHealth Reform
**Current: $22.2B (+14.8% growth) | SAVES: $800M+**

- Annual eligibility verification (currently 2.3M enrolled)
- Audit for duplicate and fraudulent claims
- Pharmacy benefit management reform
- Require asset test for non-disabled adults
- Work requirements for able-bodied adults without dependents

### SOLUTION 5: MBTA Efficiency
**Payroll: $877M | OT: $122M | SAVES: $150M+**

- Cap overtime at industry-standard 8%
- Competitive bidding for ALL contracts over $50K
- Performance-based management contracts
- Audit pension (46% funded!) and move new hires to 401k
- Consolidate administrative functions

### SOLUTION 6: Procurement Reform
**State Contracts: $10B+/year | SAVES: $300M+**

- Require minimum 3 competitive bids for all contracts
- End no-bid contract abuse
- Public online contract database with full transparency
- Inspector General review of all contracts over $1M
- Clawback provisions for non-performance

### SOLUTION 7: Education Consolidation
**Current: 396 districts | SAVES: $400M+**

- Consolidate districts under 1,000 students
- Merge administrative functions regionally
- Standardize back-office systems statewide
- Performance-based superintendent pay
- Close chronically failing schools

### SOLUTION 8: Workforce Right-Sizing
**Payroll: $10B | SAVES: $200M+**

- 2% annual attrition-based reduction
- Hiring freeze for non-essential positions
- Consolidate IT, HR, Finance across agencies
- Eliminate deputy and assistant positions
- Early retirement incentives
